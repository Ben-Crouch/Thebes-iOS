Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
You can sign up using your email address, Google account, or Apple ID. Simply tap "Sign Up" on the login screen and follow the prompts.
How do I log a workout?
Tap the "Workouts" tab, then tap the "+" button to create a new workout. Add exercises, sets, reps, and weights as you complete them.
Can I change my weight units?
Yes! Go to Settings and select your preferred weight unit (kg or lbs). All your data will be converted automatically.
How do I follow other users?
Go to the Social tab and use "Find people" to search for users. Tap the follow button next to their name to start following them.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings, scroll down to find "Delete Account", and follow the confirmation steps. This will permanently remove all your data.
My app is crashing or not working properly
Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, please contact us at support@thebes.app with details about your device and the issue.

Getting Started

New to Thebes? Here's how to get started:

  1. Create your account and verify your email
  2. Set up your profile in Settings
  3. Log your first workout in the Workouts tab
  4. Explore the Social tab to connect with other users
  5. Track your progress in the Tracker tab
