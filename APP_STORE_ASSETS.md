## üõ†Ô∏è How to Take Screenshots

### Option 1: Using Xcode Simulator
1. Open your app in Xcode Simulator
2. Choose the device size you need
3. Cmd + S to save screenshot
4. Or use Device ‚Üí Screenshot

### Option 2: Using Physical Device
1. Take screenshots on your iPhone
2. They'll automatically be the correct size for your device
3. For other sizes, you may need to resize or use simulator

### Option 3: Using Preview (Resize)
1. Take screenshots on one device
2. Open in Preview (macOS)
3. Tools ‚Üí Adjust Size
4. Resize to required dimensions
